Sovereign AI & The Bifurcated Economy

Nations are building domestic compute capacity to ensure strategic autonomy, while heavy US export controls create a bifurcated global AI stack.

Export Controls (2026 Rules)

  • US 50% Volume Cap: Aggregate processing performance exported to China cannot exceed half of what is shipped to US customers.
  • The Chinese Response: Accelerated domestic GPU development. Huawei & SMIC projected to produce 390,000 H200-equivalent chips in 2026.
  • Bottleneck: Lack of access to high-end EUV lithography blocks China from competing effectively at 2nm/3nm nodes.
